Parlons Français was established by Sophie Franklin in 2001. We are now 6 teachers, each with our own specialisms and experiences. We teach from our homes, in Cheltenham, Gloucester and the Cotswolds, or in students' homes if they prefer. We also teach in industry and colleges.
